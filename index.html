About Namu Spaces

Where Nairobi's Soul
Meets Timeless Design

Namu Spaces is a premier interior design studio born from the heart of Nairobi. We believe great design is a conversation between a space and the people who inhabit it — blending warmth, culture, and contemporary vision into every project.

Our multidisciplinary team draws inspiration from East Africa's rich textures, colours, and craftsmanship — from Lamu's carved wood to Kitengela's glass and Kenya's natural stone — weaving them seamlessly with global design sensibilities.
